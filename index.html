Quantum Grade Lab-Grown Diamonds

Our quantum-grade lab-grown diamonds are engineered with exceptionally low nitrogen concentrations and specific NV (Nitrogen-Vacancy) center densities, crucial for pioneering quantum computing, sensing, and communication technologies. These diamonds offer unparalleled coherence times and spin properties.

  • Ultra-low nitrogen concentration ([N] < 1 ppb)
  • Controlled NV-center creation and isotopic purity
  • High crystal quality and minimal strain for extended coherence
  • Optimized for quantum sensing, QIP, and quantum networks
  • Customizable material properties for specific quantum applications

Our Technology

Appsilon Enterprise offers comprehensive end-to-end technology capabilities for lab-grown diamond production and processing.

Advanced Reactor Technology (CVD & LPHT)

Our proprietary reactors enable precise control over growth conditions, resulting in lab-grown diamonds with exceptional properties and consistency. We utilize both CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and LPHT (Low Pressure High Temperature) methods.

Diamond Seed Production

We develop and produce our own high-quality diamond seeds with carefully controlled properties, ensuring the best foundation for our lab-grown diamonds and enabling precisely oriented crystal growth.

Advanced Diamond Characterization

State-of-the-art facilities allow comprehensive analysis of lab-grown diamond properties, ensuring each product meets stringent quality standards via spectroscopic, microscopic, and electrical testing.

Precision Laser Processing of Diamonds

Our advanced laser processing enables precise cutting, shaping, and surface modification of lab-grown diamond materials to meet exact dimensional and surface requirements.
